About Me

Hi, I'm Guhannath Armstrong — a Technical Lead based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, with over 4.5 years of experience at Cognizant Technology Solutions.

I started my career in IT infrastructure and identity management, and have since evolved into a full-spectrum IT Service Management professional working on multi-cloud environments for global Pharma & Life Sciences clients.

My current work spans Change Management, Incident Management, ITSM/AIMS governance, Disaster Recovery, Restore Testing, audit coordination, and leveraging cutting-edge AI tools like Microsoft Co-pilot and Claude to drive operational efficiency.

I'm a JAMF Certified Professional and a Zscaler Zero Trust Architect — with a passion for clean processes, compliance, and building things that last.
